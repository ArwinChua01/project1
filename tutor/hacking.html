What is Hacking?

In the cyber security world, the person who is able to discover a weakness in the system and manages to exploit it to accomplish his goal either good or bad, is referred to as a hacker and the process is referred to as hacking.

Nowadays, people think that hacking is only hijacking facebook accounts or defacing websites. But in reality, hacking is more than that. Hacking is the practice of modifying the features of a system or finding a loophole, in order to accomplish a goal outside the creator's original purpose.

Due to the mass attention being given to the so-called Black Hats or Crackers from the media, the reputation of all hackers, even the good ones is damaged.

Hacking is always viewed as something illegal and shrewd. The purpose of this website is to turn the image aside cand to introduce to people the true philosophy and ethics of the elusive world of hacking. Hopefully this will clear their names and give them the social status they deserve. This site will help you know everything there is to hacking.
